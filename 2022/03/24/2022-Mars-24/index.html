2022 Mars 24

Yang
2022-03-24

The first day of lifting segregation. At noon I went to Second Canteen for lunch, it was a long but plesant walk. During the road I met some cats. And at 4 p.m., after I took some cat food, I went to feed some cats around my dormitory. These cats seemed to starve for a long time as no one could feed them during segregation.

At night, wje asked me a translation of hardronic light-by-light scattering. I didn’t know the answer as well but I searched on google and found a slide explaining this then I answered her. And our talkd ended. How I wished to have a casual talk but absolutely no.

Besides, I recalled some talks with mom during spring festival holiday. Whenever I mentioned buy something, she always said you had got money, and you could buy it. When dad asked if we needed an air conditioner, I said yes, mom said next year you installed air conditioner with your own salary. When she said about my translation e-pen, and wanted to give mine to my younger sister, I said now new e-pens with more functions are available and costed only four hundred yuan while my old ones were outdated. She said again I had money, I bought one for my sister. And she mentioned my neighbourhood whose son earned his fortune by doing business from time to time. Though she sometimes told me not to think too much and do the work I like, but I still felt she was giving me pressure. While the son of my neighbour got only a plain academic qualification, I was a doctor student at SJTU, one of the best school in Shanghai. But I have got nothing. No money, no work, and no girlfriend.

Somehow, I feel isolated, no one cares my emotional need. I wanted someone to confide my worries but I can’t. And probably this will never be satisfied. I have to go through these times myself. The only place I could trust was my own blog here, where nobody knows the domain.
